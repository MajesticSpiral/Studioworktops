Profiles

ABS Square Edge

This is the modern version of a worktop and features a straight edge and 1.5mm ABS edging that gives a sturdy, square look. The simple, cleaner lines make these worktops perfect for any contemporary or minimalist kitchen design. Additional ABS edging available to order separately.

Round Edge

Round Edge worktops feature a raw particleboard core covered with a decorative HPL laminate, making them ideal for counters in kitchens, offices and shops. Laminate edging included.
